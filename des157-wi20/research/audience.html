Albert, 56

Albert has lived in the Bay Area all his life, has been born and raised here, and continues to work and raise a household in Palo Alto. Because tech hasn't prelevant 50 so years ago, he's seen the changes in the dynamic of the space he's grown up in. He seeks to document how this change has occured, as well as keep note of the childhood Palo Alto he called home.
