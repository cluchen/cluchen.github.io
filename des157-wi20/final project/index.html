How is 1960s Silicon Valley different from 2020s Silicon Valley? How are they similar?

What brought about this influx of tech?

Silicon Valley has undergone numerous changes within the past 30 years as a result of the “tech boom” that’s exploded not only online but also in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Compared to 30 years ago, the income gap has widened—the rich are getting richer due to generous salaries in tech; the poor are getting poorer as jobs are now being outsourced, contracted, automated; the region’s middle class is smaller and the average household income is increasing.

The past few decades of growing culture has facilitated not only an digital divide, but also, a growing income / societal divide.
