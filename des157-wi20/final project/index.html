Have the changing dynamics of Silicon Valley affected you in your own life?

Silicon Valley has undergone numerous changes within the past 30 years as a result of the “tech boom” that’s exploded not only digitially, but also in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Compared to 30 years ago, the income gap has widened—the rich are getting richer due to generous salaries in tech; the poor are getting poorer as jobs are now being outsourced, contracted, automated; native residents are leaving due to rising costs, and the region’s middle class is smaller with the average household income increasing.

The past few decades of growing culture has facilitated not only an digital divide, but also, a growing income / societal divide.
