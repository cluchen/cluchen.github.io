About

I’m Cynthia, a User Researcher and Designer, also a current senior at UC Davis studying Cognitive Science and Computer Science! I have a deep love for interdisciplinary design, and it’s evident in the classes I take — a jumble of subjects from psychology to design to statistics to philosophy to mathematics. I love thinking about ambiguous problems, and understanding experiences I’ve never related to before.

This fall, I'm a UX Research Intern at HBO Max, and recently was also a UX Research Intern at Salesforce. On campus, I'm leading a growing UX community that's 38 amazing designers strong. Lastly, I’m currently finishing up my last year at Davis; feel free to reach out if you’d like to know what I’m up to or just to chat about life!
