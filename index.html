About

I’m Cynthia, a User Researcher, also a current senior at UC Davis studying Cognitive Science! I have a deep love for interdisciplinary design, and it’s evident in the classes I take — a jumble of subjects from psychology to design to computer science to statistics to philosophy to mathematics. I love thinking about ambiguous problems, and understanding experiences I’ve never related to before.

I was a UX Research Intern this past fall at HBO Max and summer at Salesforce. On campus, I led a growing UX community of budding designers and researchers (like me!). Lastly, I’m currently finishing up my last year at Davis; feel free to reach out if you’d like to know what I’m up to or just to chat about life!
