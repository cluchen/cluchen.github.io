Aha! You’ve come across my top-secret prayer page!

Or I guess, not so top-secret!

Although this may not be the most conventional of pages, or the most conventional way to ask for prayer requests, it doesn’t hurt to do so! I want to be able to encourage and care for anyone who comes across this website—whether I know you personally, whether you’re a stranger on the other side of the world, whether I’ve only just met you once—we all have something to possibly pray for. I’d love to support you through prayer to God in this way, and keep you in my thoughts as I go throughout the week!

"And pray in the Spirit on all occasions with all kinds of prayers and requests. With this in mind, be alert and always keep on praying for all the saints" (Ephesians 6:18).

I’ve created a Typeform for you to submit a prayer request!

Feel free to write whatever’s on your heart—anything from something to rejoice over or a trial you’ve been going through. You can also choose to keep things anonymous! Please feel free to do so.

I’ll be praying for you! :)
